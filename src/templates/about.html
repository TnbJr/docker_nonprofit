About Us

Our Mission

Due to the lack of resources many underprivileged children cannot attain a basic education. Our goal is to reverse the bleak future of these children by providing them with educational funding, nutritional food distribution as well as access to shelter and health care professionals.

Our Team

Dr. LaRita Woods is the founder and president of Anchored In Love International, which was birthed in 2015. By Profession Dr. Woods is a practicing Dentist of 30 years in Brooklyn, NY. She has spent many years participating in dental missions as well as evangelical missions in Africa, Europe, Central America, and the West Indies.

Why?

After traveling through the village of Kirombe of Nsambya, Kampala Uganda, Dr Woods was deeply moved by the many orphans who have lost their parents to AIDS or who had been simply abandoned by their parents. Knowing that with some dedication and organization lives could be changed Dr Woods set out on a mission to help enrich the lives of underprivileged children across the globe.
